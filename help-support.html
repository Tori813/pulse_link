Account Help

  • Creating an account

    Creating an Account

    How to Create Your Account:

    1. Click 'Sign Up' in the top right corner
    2. Enter your email and create a password
    3. Verify your email address
    4. Complete your profile information
    5. Set up two-factor authentication

    Requirements:

    • Must be 18+ years old
    • Valid email address required
    • Strong password (min. 8 characters)

    Already have an account? Sign in here

  • Updating profile information

    Updating Profile

    How to Update Your Profile:

    1. Go to 'My Profile' in the main menu
    2. Click 'Edit' next to the section to update
    3. Make your changes and click 'Save'
    4. Upload a new profile picture if desired
    5. Verify changes with your password

    Information You Can Update:

    • Personal details (name, date of birth)
    • Contact information
    • Password and security settings
    • Notification preferences

    Some changes may require verification.

Facilities

  • Finding healthcare facilities

    Finding Healthcare Facilities

    How to Find Healthcare Facilities:

    1. Search by Location: Enter your city, town, or postal code in the search bar to find facilities near you.
    2. Filter by Type: Narrow down results by facility type (hospital, clinic, pharmacy, etc.) or by the specific service you need.
    3. View on Map: See all nearby facilities plotted on an interactive map with distance indicators.
    4. Check Availability: Real-time availability of services and estimated wait times when available.
    5. Get Directions: One-click access to turn-by-turn navigation to your chosen facility.

    Tips:

    • Save your frequently visited facilities for quick access
    • Check operating hours before visiting
    • Verify insurance acceptance if applicable

    For urgent medical concerns, please contact emergency services immediately.

  • Viewing facility details

    Viewing Facility Details

    What You Can See:

    1. Basic Information: Facility name, type, and contact details
    2. Services Offered: List of available medical services and specialties
    3. Operating Hours: Regular hours and holiday schedules
    4. Insurance Accepted: List of accepted insurance providers
    5. Provider Directory: Doctors and specialists available at the facility

    Additional Details:

    • Patient reviews and ratings
    • Wait times (if available)
    • Accessibility features
    • Parking and transportation options
    • COVID-19 safety measures

    Click on any facility to view its complete profile and available services.

  • Saving favorite locations

    Saving Favorite Facilities

    How to Save Facilities:

    1. Find a Facility: Search for the healthcare facility you want to save
    2. Click the Heart Icon: Look for the ♥ or 'Save' button on the facility's profile
    3. Access Your Favorites: Find all saved locations in 'My Favorites' section
    4. Organize: Create custom folders for different types of facilities
    5. Get Notified: Enable notifications for updates about your saved facilities

    Benefits of Saving:

    • Quick access to frequently visited providers
    • Receive updates about your saved facilities
    • Compare services and wait times easily
    • Share locations with family members

    You can save up to 20 facilities. Manage your saved locations

  • Facility ratings and reviews

    Facility Ratings & Reviews

    How to Use Ratings:

    1. View Overall Ratings: See the average star rating for each facility
    2. Read Reviews: Browse feedback from other patients
    3. Filter Reviews: Sort by date, rating, or most helpful
    4. Check Specific Ratings: See scores for cleanliness, staff, wait times, etc.
    5. Write a Review: Share your experience after visiting

    What to Look For:

    • Overall patient satisfaction scores
    • Comments about wait times and scheduling
    • Feedback on staff professionalism
    • Facility cleanliness and amenities
    • Recent reviews for current conditions

    Your feedback helps others make informed decisions. Leave a review after your visit.

  • Booking appointments

    Booking Appointments

    How to Book an Appointment:

    1. Select 'Book Appointment' from the dashboard
    2. Choose your healthcare provider or specialty
    3. Select an available date and time
    4. Enter reason for visit and any notes
    5. Confirm your appointment details

    What You'll Need:

    • Your insurance information (if applicable)
    • Preferred date and time
    • Reason for your visit
    • Any relevant medical history

Medical Records

  • Accessing medical history

    Accessing Medical History

    How to View Your Medical History:

    1. Log in to your patient portal
    2. Navigate to 'Medical Records'
    3. Select 'Medical History'
    4. Filter by date or visit type
    5. View detailed visit summaries

    What You Can See:

    • Past diagnoses and conditions
    • Medication history
    • Allergies and immunizations
    • Lab and test results
    • Procedure history

    For records older than 5 years, please contact support.

  • Sharing records with providers

    Sharing Medical Records

    How to Share Your Records:

    1. Go to 'Medical Records'
    2. Select 'Share Records'
    3. Choose which records to share
    4. Enter provider's information
    5. Set access duration and permissions

    Important Information:

    • You control who sees your records
    • Access can be revoked at any time
    • View sharing history and status
    • Set up automatic sharing with primary care

    Your privacy is protected by HIPAA regulations.

  • Downloading records

    Downloading Records

    How to Download Your Records:

    1. Navigate to 'Medical Records'
    2. Select 'Download Records'
    3. Choose date range and record types
    4. Select format (PDF, CCDA, etc.)
    5. Click 'Download' and save securely

    Available Formats:

    • PDF (human-readable)
    • CCDA (for other providers)
    • Text/CSV (for personal use)
    • DICOM (for imaging studies)

    Large files may take time to prepare. You'll receive an email when ready.

  • Understanding test results

    Understanding Test Results

    How to Read Your Results:

    1. Results are typically available within 24-72 hours
    2. Look for 'Normal' or 'Abnormal' indicators
    3. Reference ranges are provided for context
    4. Compare with previous results if available
    5. Contact your provider with any questions

    Common Test Types:

    • Blood tests (CBC, metabolic panel, etc.)
    • Imaging results (X-rays, MRIs, etc.)
    • Pathology reports
    • Specialty tests (genetic, allergy, etc.)

    For abnormal results, your provider will contact you. Learn more about common tests.

    Account Security

    How to Secure Your Account:

    1. Create a strong, unique password
    2. Enable two-factor authentication
    3. Regularly update your password

    Authentication Methods:

    • Email verification
